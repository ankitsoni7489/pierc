Who We Are

The Entrepreneurship Club at Parul University, an initiative by PIERC, is your launchpad for entrepreneurial success. We empower students to innovate, collaborate, and turn ideas into reality through workshops, events, and mentorship.

Connected to PIERC’s startup ecosystem, we provide access to resources, industry leaders, and opportunities like the Vadodara Startup Demo Day. Ready to shape the future? Join us!
